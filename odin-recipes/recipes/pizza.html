Pizza Recipe

Pizza is a beloved Italian dish originating from Naples, consisting of a thin, round dough base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various ingredients, then baked at high heat for a crispy crust and melted toppings.

Ingredients

  • Flour (3 cups), yeast (1 packet), warm water (1 cup), olive oil (2 tbsp), salt, sugar
  • Tomato sauce (1 cup), mozzarella cheese (2 cups shredded), pepperoni or veggies, fresh basil, oregano, Parmesan
  • Cornmeal for dusting, optional meats like sausage or veggies like peppers/onions

Steps

  1. Make Dough: Mix flour, yeast, water, oil, salt, and sugar; knead 10 minutes, let rise 1 hour until doubled.
  2. Prep & Assemble: Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C) with pizza stone. Roll dough thin, spread sauce, add cheese and toppings.
  3. Bake: Slide onto stone, bake 12-15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese bubbles; slice after 5 minutes rest.
