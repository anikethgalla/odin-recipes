Cheese Sandwich Recipe

A cheese sandwich (or grilled cheese) is a simple, comforting dish made by sandwiching melted cheese between buttery, toasted bread slices for a crispy exterior and gooey interior.

Ingredients

  • Sliced bread (4 pieces), cheddar or American cheese (4-6 slices)
  • 2-4 tbsp softened for spreading
  • Tomato slices, ham, mustard, or herbs for flavor

Steps

  1. Prep: Butter one side of each bread slice; layer 2-3 cheese slices between unbuttered sides of two slices.
  2. Cook: Heat skillet over medium; cook sandwich butter-side down 2-3 minutes per side until golden and cheese melts, pressing down.
  3. Serve: Slice diagonally and enjoy hot; rest 1 minute if adding fillings.
